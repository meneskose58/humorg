# Privacy Policy for Humorg

**Last updated:** 2024-02-17

This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is collected, used, and shared when you use the **Humorg** application (the "Service").

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Provide to Us
- **Account Information:** When you register for an account, we collect your email address and name to identify you within the application.
- **Home Management Data:** We collect the data you input into the application, including tasks, expense records, requests, and other shared information necessary for the app to function as a home management tool.

### 1.2 Information Collected Automatically
- **Device Information:** We may collect information about your mobile device, including the hardware model, operating system version, unique device identifiers, and mobile network information.
- **Usage Data:** We collect information about your interactions with the Service, such as the features you use and the time spent on the application.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:
- Provide, maintain, and improve the **Humorg** application.
- Facilitate communication and coordination between home members (e.g., assigning tasks, sharing expenses).
- Send you technical notices, updates, security alerts, and support messages via push notifications.
- Monitor and analyze trends, usage, and activities in connection with our Service.

## 3. Sharing of Information

We process your data using third-party services to ensure the functionality of the app:

### 3.1 Service Providers
- **Supabase:** We use Supabase as our backend database and authentication provider. Your data is stored securely on their servers. You can review their privacy policy [here](https://supabase.com/privacy).
- **Firebase (Google):** We use Firebase messaging services to send notifications to your device. You can review Google's privacy policy [here](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

### 3.2 Legal Requirements
We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in the good faith belief that such action is necessary to comply with a legal obligation, protect and defend our rights or property, or protect the personal safety of users of the Service or the public.

## 4. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to help protect information about you from loss, theft, misuse, and unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction. Your sensitive data, such as authentication tokens, is stored using secure storage mechanisms on your device.

## 5. Your Choices

### 5.1 Account Information
You may update, correct, or delete information about you at any time by logging into your account or contacting us. If you wish to delete your account entirely, please use the in-app deletion feature or contact us directly.

### 5.2 Push Notifications
We may send you push notifications. You can opt-out of receiving these notifications at any time by changing the settings on your mobile device.

## 6. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not directed to anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from anyone under the age of 13.

## 7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
